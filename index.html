Tumble log like 2005

A domain to delegate your tumblr too to stop it being under tumblr.com.

Take these steps:

  1. Go to www.tumblr.com/settings/blog
  2. Click on the pencil to the right of your name
  3. Change the custom domain to [yourname].tumblelog.xyz
  4. Click test
  5. Click save
  6. Go to http://[yourname].tumblelog.xyz

Example tumble log: kevinmarks.tumblelog.xyz formerly kevinmarks.tumblr.com

The name "Tumble log" created by Jason Kottke in 2005
